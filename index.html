Introduction to LMCC

The Localization Management Core Competencies (LMCC) typology is a hierarchy of localization knowledge and skills necessary for the professional practice of localization management. The LMCC typology is comprised of seven dimensions, each with related competencies and skills.

This self-evaluation tool will help you assess your strengths and areas for improvement across these dimensions, providing insight into your localization management competencies.

The Seven Dimensions

  • Language, Culture, & Communication - Bilingualism, Cultural awareness, Effective communication
  • Research & Critical Thinking - Analytical skills, Independent learning, Feedback and evaluation
  • Market Awareness - Industry knowledge, Standards, Organizations, Market trends
  • Subject Matter Expertise - Data security, Legal knowledge, Industry-specific knowledge
  • GILT - Globalization, Internationalization, Localization, and Translation
  • Technology - General tech skills, Communication platforms, PM tools, CAT, DTP, Software/Games/Websites, MT, and more
  • Management - Stakeholder, Account, Project, Quality, Terminology, Production/Operations, and Program
